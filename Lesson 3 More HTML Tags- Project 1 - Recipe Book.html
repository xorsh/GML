French Toast

This meal can be served for breakfast or brunch and is very filling to get you started for the day.

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Large eggs 6
Vanilla 2 tbsp
Nutmeg 1 pinch
Bread 6 slices
Vegetable oil 4 tbsp
milk 1 1/2 cups
Cinnamon 1/2 tsp
Butter 4 tbsp
Maple Syrup For serving
  1. Whisk your eggs, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a medium bowl.
  2. Put your bread slices in a shallow dish so that they are in a single layer (not piled up). Pour the egg mixture over the bread and allow it to soak fo 10 minutes. Turn slices over and repeat (about 10 minutes more).
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons butter and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry half the bread slices until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side.

Try serving with fresh berries and cream.

Source: Martha Stewart's Classic French Toast

Pizza

A delicious italian meal that is great to share with friends or family!

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantities
Naan 1 package
Tomato sauce 1 can
Mozzarella 1 brick
Pepperoni 1 stick
  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees
  2. Put your naan bread on a cookie sheet.
  3. Using a spoon, spread the tomato sauce onto the naan bread.
  4. Grate your cheese and sprinkle over the pizza.
  5. Slice up your pepperoni stick and place on top of the pizza
  6. Put your pizzas in the oven for 5 minutes or until cheese starts to bubble.

Serve warm and enjoy!

Cookies

A great simple desert to fix your sweet tooth

  • Time: 40 min
  • Makes: 40 cookies
Ingredients Quantities
Butter 1 cup
Egg 1
Brown sugar 2/3 cups
White sugar 2/3 cups
Vanilla 1 tsp
Flour 2 cup
Baking soda 1/2 tsp
Chocolate chips 1 cup
  1. Preheat oven to 365 degrees
  2. Blend butter, white sugar and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla.
  4. Mix in the flour and baking soda.
  5. Mix in the chocolate chips.
  6. Grease a baking sheet
  7. Using a spoon, create balls of a width of 1 inch. Place 15 cookies per pan
  8. Cook the cookies for 8 minutes. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before serving.

Goes great with a glass of milk.
